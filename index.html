Liturgy - Djennaration (Black Metal)

Djennaration is a brutal 8 minute epic from New York black metal/blackgaze outfit Liturgy.
The song's thunderous instrumentation occasionally broken up by digital drum breaks, and shrill vocals from
marvelously talented Haela Hunt-Hendrix, make this song a chaotic yet beautiful cacophony of sounds. This song
is a must-listen for any black metal fan who hasn't yet heard it. Listen here.

Lord Juco - Natural Selection (Hip hop)

The intro cut from rapper Lord Juco's latest EP "Company II" is a short but sweet listen.
The brass sample based beat immediately grabs your attention. That coupled with a smooth flow
clearly inspired by the Griselda label makes this song a great lesser known pick for fans of that
style who are looking for more like it. Listen here.
