About Me

Welcome to Underground Sound! My name is Andrew. I made this website to share music
from lesser-known artists to expose their music to more people. I hope to expand these artists' fanbases
and put people on to music they may have never heard of. I am a huge music fan and finding great underground artists
is always a special moment for me. I'm hoping to share these special moments with others.
Some of my favorite artists are System of a Down, Ween, and Kendrick Lamar. I've included a collage of my
all-time top 5 favorite albums. I really hope you enjoy the music I share here!

My Top 5 Favorite Albums:

  1. Toxicity - System of a Down
  2. Yeezus - Kanye West
  3. The Pod - Ween
  4. DAMN. - Kendrick Lamar
  5. White Pony - Deftones
